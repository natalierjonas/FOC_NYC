What are "flags of convenience"?

Dozens of nations allow their flags to be registered and flown by ships originating from other countries, allowing them to skirt more stringent labor, taxation and environmental requirements.

How are they used?

FoCs allow illegal maritime activities (such as drug smuggling and human trafficking) to proliferate, especially in the fishing industry. Ships can operate in international waters without a traceable identity, leaving subsequent crimes unpunished. Most commonly, FoCs are used in fishing, supplying New Yorkers with all sorts of seafood.
